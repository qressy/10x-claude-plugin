Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

The 10x Shopping ("the Service") is a Claude plugin that connects to a Shopify platform backend to help users browse products, manage carts, and checkout. This policy describes what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Shopping interactions: Search queries, product views, cart contents, and tool calls are logged by the backend for analytics and debugging.
  • No personal data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information through the plugin. Any personal data provided during checkout is handled directly by Shopify.
  • Conversation context: Claude may retain conversation history per Anthropic's policies. We do not have access to your conversations.

How We Use Data

  • To process your shopping requests (search, browse, cart, checkout)
  • To generate aggregate analytics (popular products, search trends)
  • To improve the service

Data Retention

Tool call event logs are retained for 30 days and then archived. Archived events are periodically deleted.

Third Parties

  • Shopify: Product data is fetched from Shopify stores via their Storefront API and Global Catalog. Purchases are completed on Shopify's platform under their privacy policy.
  • Anthropic: This plugin runs on Anthropic's Claude platform under their privacy policy.

Payment Data

We never process or store payment information. All payments are handled securely by Shopify's checkout system.
